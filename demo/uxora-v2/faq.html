Can AI SEO software improve my search rankings?

Yes! By automating and optimizing SEO efforts, the software helps identify opportunities, fix issues, and execute strategies that boost your website’s ranking on search engines.

How does it handle keyword research?

AI SEO software identifies high-impact keywords based on search volume, competition, and relevance. It also suggests trending and long-tail keywords to target niche audiences.

Is it suitable for beginners?

Absolutely. Many AI SEO tools offer user-friendly interfaces, step-by-step recommendations, and automated processes, making them accessible for users with little to no SEO experience.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my account after purchase?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your subscription at any time from the in-app billing section under the change plan button.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit cards including Visa, MasterCard, Discover, American Express and UnionPay, wire transfer (upon request), check or money order (US only, upon request)

Am I charged when I enter my credit card?

If I have an annual plan, does my credit reset annually or monthly?

Your credit resets annually or after you change your plan (upgrades and downgrades).

Does it support multiple websites?

Yes, most AI SEO software supports multi-site management, making it ideal for agencies or businesses with several websites.

Can it create content for my website?

Some AI SEO tools include content generation features that suggest or create optimized content, but you may still need to refine it for tone and brand voice.
